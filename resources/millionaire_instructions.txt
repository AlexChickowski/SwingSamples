Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? is a multiple choice trivia game show.  You will
be asked up to 15 questions.  Each questions that you answer correctly will
move you up a level on the prize ladder.  Answer a question and you may lose
some of your earnings.

The $1000 and $32000 are safety net amounts.  Once you get to these amounts,
you are guaranteed these earnings.  If you get a questions wrong you loose
everything except these safety net amounts.  At any point, you have the option
of walking away with your current earnings (see the Game menu).  If you answer
all 15 questions correctly, then you win a million dollars!

DISCLAIMER: You will not actually win any money playing this game!

NOTE: This version of the game does not include lifelines.
